Mexican Candies Cheat Sheet

A guide to the candies you're about to taste!

🍫 Sweet / Chocolatey

🍓

Bubulubu Mini

Marshmallow and strawberry jelly covered in chocolate. Soft, fluffy, and sweet.

Soft, fluffy, and sweet
🥜

Tin Larín Mini

Wafer with peanut cream and chocolate. Crunchy and nutty-sweet.

Crunchy and nutty-sweet
🍫

Bocadín

Wafer bar with peanut filling covered in chocolate. Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat.

Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat
🌽

Kranky

Crunchy cornflake clusters covered in chocolate. Crispy, sweet, and crunchy.

Crispy, sweet, and crunchy
🌿

Chocoreta

Mini mint chocolate candies. Like minty M&Ms, cool and sweet.

Like minty M&Ms, cool and sweet

🍯 Creamy / Nutty

🥄

Duvalín Trisabor

Creamy hazelnut, vanilla, and strawberry spread. Like Nutella pudding, eaten with a lil spoon.

Like Nutella pudding, eaten with a lil spoon
🥜

Mazapán de la Rosa

Crumbly peanut candy. Melts instantly, very sweet and nutty.

Melts instantly, very sweet and nutty
🍭

Paleta de Cajeta Coronado

Goat's milk caramel lollipop. Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat.

Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat
🍯

Mini Gloria

Goat's milk caramel candy. Creamy, smooth, and very sweet.

Creamy, smooth, and very sweet
🥜

Glorias (Las Sevillanas)

Goat's milk caramel with pecans. Soft, rich, and nutty.

Soft, rich, and nutty

🍓 Fruity / Sweet

🍺

Paleta Vero Cerveza

Beer-shaped lollipop. Sweet caramel-like flavor, no spice.

Sweet caramel-like flavor, no spice
❤️

Paleta Vero Corazón

Heart-shaped strawberry lollipop. Fruity, sweet, and classic.

Fruity, sweet, and classic
🤡

Paleta Payaso Mini

Marshmallow lollipop with chocolate shell and gummy eyes. Sweet, soft, and sugary.

Sweet, soft, and sugary
🐻

Panditas

Fruity gummy bears. Soft and sweet.

Soft and sweet
🍬

Dulci Gomas

Sugar-coated fruit gummies. Chewy and very sweet.

Chewy and very sweet
🇲🇽

Cocada (Mexican flag bar)

Chewy coconut candy in green, white, red. Sweet and tropical.

Sweet and tropical

🌶️ Sweet + Spicy / Tangy

🧴

Pelón Pelo Rico

Tangy tamarind paste you squeeze out. Sweet, sour, and chili-spicy.

Sweet, sour, and chili-spicy
🧡

Rellerindo (orange)

Chamoy-watermelon tamarind candy. Spicy, chewy and bold.

Spicy, chewy and bold
🔴

Rellerindo (red)

Classic tamarind candy with soft spicy-sweet center. Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat.

Light and sweet, like a Mexican KitKat
🥁

Tamborines

Pressed sugar discs with chili. Crunchy, sugary, and lightly spicy.

Crunchy, sugary, and lightly spicy
🍓

Picafresa

Strawberry gummy coated with chili sugar. Sweet, tangy, and mildly spicy.

Sweet, tangy, and mildly spicy
🐉

Dragonzitos

Fizzy fruity powder with a chili twist. Tangy/fizzy sparkle.

Tangy/fizzy sparkle
👆

Chupadedo

Finger-shaped lollipop with eucalyptus-mint flavor and chili — cooling, spicy, and unique.

Cooling, spicy, and unique
🌶️

Trabalenguas

Layered fruit and chili — spicy, sour, and sweet as you go.

Spicy, sour, and sweet as you go
🥭

Mango Picante

Mango with chili — tropical, tangy, and spicy-sweet.

Tropical, tangy, and spicy-sweet
🌽

Elote Picante

Corn-shaped with chili — sweet, salty, and spicy.

Sweet, salty, and spicy
🍉

Sandi Brochas Rellena

Watermelon with chili and spicy center — juicy and hot.

Juicy and hot
🔥

Takis Fuego

Inspired by Takis chips — spicy, sour, and chili-lime flavored.

Spicy, sour, and chili-lime flavored

Enjoy these authentic Mexican flavors! 🇲🇽
